Loneliness by Province

Summary

Using data obtained from Statistics Canada, the purpose of this project is to determine whether geography and gender influence feelings of loneliness. The data was collected through a Canadian Social Survey (CSS). The purpose of these studies is to gain insight in to social issues. These studies are conducted on a quarterly basis. The data represented below was collected on Q1 of 2022. This data only represents the Canadian provinces; territories were not included in this research.
